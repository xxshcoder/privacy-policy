Privacy Policy for QuizX

Effective Date: April 17, 2025

Introduction

Welcome to QuizX ("we," "us," "our," or the "Company"). QuizX is designed to provide users with an engaging and interactive platform to test their knowledge across various topics through quizzes. We are committed to protecting your privacy and ensuring that your personal information is handled in a safe, transparent, and lawful manner. This Privacy Policy outlines how we collect, use, store, disclose, and protect your personal information when you use our QuizX (the "App"). By accessing or using the App, you agree to the terms of this Privacy Policy.

Our mission is to create a fun and competitive environment where users can participate in quizzes and compare their performance with others via a public leaderboard. To enable this functionality, we collect limited personal information through Google Login, specifically your name and email address. This Privacy Policy is intended to provide you with a clear understanding of our data practices, your rights, and the measures we take to safeguard your information. We encourage you to read this document carefully and contact us with any questions or concerns.

This Privacy Policy applies to all users of the App, regardless of their location, and complies with applicable data protection laws, including but not limited to the General Data Protection Regulation (GDPR) for users in the European Union and the California Consumer Privacy Act (CCPA) for residents of California. We have structured this policy to be comprehensive, addressing various aspects of data handling to ensure transparency and trust.

1. Information We Collect

1.1 Personal Information

When you sign in to QuizX using your Google account, we collect the following personal information:

  • Name: We collect your name as provided by your Google account. This name is used to identify you on the App's public leaderboard, which displays quiz rankings for all users.
  • Email Address: We collect your email address as provided by your Google account. This is used to associate your quiz progress, scores, and account activities with your unique profile and to communicate with you when necessary.

We rely on Google Login to authenticate users securely and streamline the sign-in process. By using Google Login, you authorize us to access and collect the aforementioned information from your Google account. We do not collect additional personal information, such as your date of birth, gender, or other profile details, unless explicitly provided by you in communications with us.

1.2 Non-Personal Information

In addition to personal information, we may collect non-personal information that does not directly identify you. This includes:

  • Usage Data: Information about how you interact with the App, such as the quizzes you take, your scores, the time spent on the App, and the features you use.
  • Device Information: Technical details about the device you use to access the App, such as the operating system, browser type, IP address, and device identifiers.
  • Aggregated Data: Statistical or demographic data derived from user interactions, which is anonymized and used for analytical purposes.

Non-personal information helps us understand user behavior, improve the App's performance, and enhance the overall user experience. This data is typically collected automatically through cookies, analytics tools, or other tracking technologies, as described in Section 6.

1.3 Information You Provide Voluntarily

If you contact us directly (e.g., via email or through a support form), you may provide additional information, such as feedback, inquiries, or complaints. This information is used solely to address your request and is stored securely in accordance with this Privacy Policy.

2. How We Use Your Information

We use the information we collect for various purposes to provide, maintain, and improve QuizX. Below are the primary ways we use your personal and non-personal information:

2.1 To Provide and Operate the App

Your name is displayed on the App's leaderboard to showcase your quiz rankings and foster a competitive environment. The leaderboard is a core feature of the App, allowing users to see how they perform compared to others. Your email address is used to link your quiz scores, progress, and account activities to your unique profile, ensuring a personalized experience.

We process your personal information to authenticate your identity during sign-in, manage your account, and deliver the App's core functionalities. For example, when you complete a quiz, your score is associated with your account and reflected on the leaderboard under your name.

2.2 To Communicate with You

We may use your email address to send you important updates, such as changes to the App, updates to this Privacy Policy, or responses to your inquiries. These communications are limited to essential information and are not used for marketing purposes unless you explicitly opt in to receive promotional content (if applicable).

2.3 To Improve and Analyze the App

We use non-personal information, such as usage data and aggregated analytics, to understand how users interact with the App. This helps us identify popular features, detect technical issues, and develop new quizzes or functionalities. For example, we may analyze which quiz categories are most popular to create more engaging content.

Personal information, such as your name and email address, is not used for analytics unless it is anonymized and aggregated with other users' data. This ensures that your identity remains protected while we gain insights to enhance the App.

2.4 To Ensure Security and Compliance

We use your information to monitor and prevent unauthorized access, fraud, or abuse of the App. For instance, we may use your email address to verify your identity if you request account deletion or report a security issue. We also use device information to detect and address potential security threats, such as suspicious login attempts.

In addition, we may use your information to comply with legal obligations, such as responding to lawful requests from regulatory authorities or maintaining records as required by applicable data protection laws.

3. How We Share Your Information

We are committed to protecting your personal information and do not sell, trade, or rent it to third parties for marketing purposes. However, we may share your information in the following limited circumstances:

3.1 Public Leaderboard

Your name, as provided by your Google account, is displayed on the App's public leaderboard, which is visible to all users of the App. This is an integral part of the App's competitive functionality, allowing users to see their rankings and compare their performance with others. By using the App, you consent to your name being publicly displayed on the leaderboard. If you do not wish for your name to appear, you may choose not to use the App or request account deletion (see Section 5).

3.2 Service Providers

We may share your personal information with trusted third-party service providers who assist us in operating the App. These providers perform services such as:

  • Hosting and maintaining the App's servers and databases.
  • Providing analytics tools to track usage and performance.
  • Processing email communications or support requests.

All service providers are contractually obligated to protect your information, use it only for the purposes we specify, and comply with applicable data protection laws. We carefully select service providers with strong privacy and security practices to ensure your information remains safe.

3.3 Legal and Regulatory Requirements

We may disclose your personal information if required to do so by law or in response to valid legal requests, such as:

  • Court orders, subpoenas, or warrants.
  • Requests from government agencies or law enforcement.
  • Investigations into suspected illegal activities or violations of our Terms of Use.

In such cases, we will disclose only the information necessary to comply with the legal obligation and will take steps to protect your privacy to the extent permitted by law.

3.4 Business Transfers

In the event of a merger, acquisition, or sale of all or part of our assets, your personal information may be transferred to the acquiring entity. We will notify you of any such transfer and provide you with options to manage your information, such as opting out or requesting deletion, as applicable.

4. Data Security

We take the security of your personal information seriously and implement reasonable technical, administrative, and physical measures to protect it from unauthorized access, disclosure, alteration, or destruction. These measures include:

  • Encryption: We use encryption protocols (e.g., SSL/TLS) to secure data transmitted between your device and our servers.
  • Access Controls: We restrict access to your personal information to authorized personnel who need it to perform their duties.
  • Regular Security Audits: We conduct periodic reviews of our systems and practices to identify and address potential vulnerabilities.
  • Secure Storage: Your personal information is stored on secure servers hosted by reputable cloud service providers.
